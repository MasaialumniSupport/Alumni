Financial & Contingency Support

Explore the financial resources available to you as a Masai alumnus, from emergency aid to loan guidance. This section details the eligibility criteria and the process for availing support.

Eligibility for EMI Support

We are here to support you during difficult times. You are eligible for EMI support if you meet any of the following criteria:

  • Company Shutdown: If your company goes out of business and winds up operations.
  • Company Layoffs: If you have experienced a layoff from your previous employer.
  • Major Pay Cuts: If you have suffered significant pay cuts that have impacted your financial stability.
Steps to Avail Financial Support

To avail financial support for EMI payments, you must complete the following steps. Please note, this support is contingent on your active participation in the placement process.

Requests submitted before the 25th of the month will be processed for the current month's EMI support after verification. Requests after the 25th will be considered for the next month.

Crucial: EMI/Contingency support will only be provided once you have completed the placement support steps within the 7-day window and your physical documents have been received.
  1. Fill & Submit the Alumni Contingency Form: Reach out to the alumni support team to receive the form. The team will evaluate your request and respond within two weeks.
  2. Submit Required Documents: Send the following physical documents to our office address before the 1st of the month for which you need support:
    • Filled and signed contingency application form
    • Signed copies of the cheques
    • Signed copy of the Letter of Intent

Placement & Career Support

Our structured placement process is designed to help you land your ideal job. The steps below detail the path from preparing your profile to receiving an offer. Completing these steps is crucial for accessing placement and financial support.

Part A: Resume, Portfolio & GitHub (RPG) Clearance

Step 1: Update your RPG. Ensure your Resume, Portfolio, and GitHub are current and reflect your latest skills and projects.

Step 2: Submit the form. Complete the RPG form and upload your updated documents.

Step 3: Review the guidelines. For detailed instructions on RPG clearance, please refer to the RPG Clearance Steps.

Part B: Hukumu Interview & Practice Interviews

Platform: Hukumu Interview Platform

Objective: This is a human-based interview where you must score a minimum of 5 marks.

Outcome:
  • Score below 5: You'll be added to the Continued Curriculum for further skill enhancement.
  • Score 5 or above: You'll receive placement opportunities and be part of the Continued Curriculum.

Practice: We recommend using Placed.tech for mock interviews and skill-based evaluations.

Part C: Alumni Code of Conduct

Review: Please go through the Masai Code of Conduct.

Acknowledgment: You must sign and submit this document as part of the RPG form.

Important Notes

  • Process Duration: The completion of these steps takes a maximum of 7 days. Failure to complete them within this timeframe will result in ineligibility for EMI and Placement support.
  • Support Suspension: Delays may lead to the suspension of your support.
  • Workspace Transition: Upon starting placement support, you'll be moved to the Placement workspace on Slack.

Scam Alert & Financial Fraud

Fraudsters are contacting students with fake "one-time settlement" offers. Your security is our priority. This section provides critical steps to verify requests and protect yourself from financial fraud.

Before making any payment, always follow these two essential steps:

  • 1.
    Ask for an email: Ensure it's sent directly from an official @masaischool.com address.
  • 2.
    Verify on Slack: Reach out on our official Slack channel to confirm the request.
Questions to Ask a Suspicious Caller

Scammers don‚Äôt have your detailed information. Use these questions to verify their identity:

  • "What is my exact loan amount?"
  • "How many EMIs have I paid, and how many are pending?"
  • "What is my student code at Masai School?"
  • "What is my ISA/PAP agreement signing date?"
  • "Can you send me a message from the official Masai School Slack platform?"

Only proceed if these questions are answered satisfactorily.

Critical Warning & RBI Guidelines

DO NOT make any payments unless verified. Ensure payments are made directly to Nolan Edutech or your respective NBFC bank account.

If you receive a suspicious call, report it using this form.
